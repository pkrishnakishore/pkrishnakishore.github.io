Identified Gaps (Needing Solutions) [+]

Full-text Screening (Initial Maturity):

Fewer validation studies vs. TiAb screening; accuracy, reproducibility, context understanding limit practical application.

Certainty of Evidence (GRADE) (Initial Maturity):

AI use minimal; no established automation tools; manual methods prevail; no validated LLM approach.

Citation Retrieval (Initial Maturity):

NLP tools for queries in development; uncertain performance/precision; not optimized for grey lit.

Dynamic Validation Framework (Initial Maturity):

Standardized framework needed; current evaluations use varied/private criteria; selection often subjective; lack of structured risk assessment.

Evidence Integration and Reporting (Initial Maturity):

No standardized workflows/tools for integrated synthesis; AI lacks features to contextualize/visualize/translate results for policymakers.

Citizen Engagement (Initial Maturity):

Lack of tracking for public use; limited public awareness of AI biases; engagement underdeveloped (esp. Global South); digital divides hinder access.
